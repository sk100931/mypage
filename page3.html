What is a Labrador

The Labrador Retriever, or simply Labrador, is a British breed of retriever gun dog. It was developed in the United Kingdom from St. John's water dogs imported from the colony of Newfoundland, and was named after the Labrador region of that colony.

What is a life span of a Labrador

Labradors are generally healthy dogs that live 10-12 years on average. However, they do have some health issues that can cause them to die at a younger age. The main causes of death in Labradors are cancer, heatstroke, epilepsy, heart disease and bloat (gastric torsion).
